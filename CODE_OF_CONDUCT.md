# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Talamus contributors, maintainers, and users commit to making participation in
the project respectful and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of
experience, identity, background, ability, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind, specific, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code, never people.
- Assume good intent while staying open to correction.
- Respect privacy, consent, and the local-first values of the project.
- Disclose conflicts of interest and security concerns responsibly.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, deliberate
intimidation, doxxing, sustained disruption, or publishing another person's
private information without consent are not tolerated.

## Scope

This code applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, release and
community spaces, and whenever someone represents Talamus publicly.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, warnings, or access when behavior
violates this code. Report abuse privately through GitHub's reporting tools; if
the incident involves a maintainer, use GitHub's abuse-reporting channel. Good
faith reports will be handled as confidentially as practical, without
retaliation.
