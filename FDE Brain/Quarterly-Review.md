# Quarterly Review

A scheduled ceremony (at minimum once per quarter) where an operator walks through every item in the `review/needs-human` queue and makes a deliberate decision: promote to the wiki, discard, or send back for refinement.

The purpose is twofold: it prevents the [[Failure Review Queue]] from becoming a graveyard of forgotten items, and it forces a periodic reality check on pipeline quality. If the queue is consistently large, that is a signal to improve extraction or normalization upstream.
