# Fine-Grained Citation

A citation that points to a **heading anchor or paragraph** within a source, not merely to the file or document. Fine-grained citations let a human jump straight to the relevant passage when verifying a model-generated claim.

Contrast with coarse citation (e.g., `source: report.pdf`) which forces the reader to re-scan the entire document. In a wiki context, link to the specific `#anchor` whenever possible.
